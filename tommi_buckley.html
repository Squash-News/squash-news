The West Totton Astra Dome:
Venue of Legends



The West Totton Astra Dome is the UK's premier arena for seeing top, top quality players, exhibiting world class squash. Former UK No.1, Peter Nicol is quoted as saying "If I only have one regret in my international career, it's that I never got to play at The West Totton Astra Dome".



Come and see what all the fuss is about. On Thursday evenings there is said to be no hotter venue. Reserve a court on 02380 861712 or come and catch the action at Hazel Farm Road, Totton, Southampton, Hampshire, SO40 8WU.




The Iceman cometh. Every Squash Championship needs a hardman, and they don't come much harder than Tombo 'Iceman' Buckley.
Hailing from the frozen wastelands of Finland, Tommi was raised by wolves and lived solely on a diet of pinecones and wild boar, until he was taken in by Finnish Squash Champion Juha Fioskjordi, in 1986.
It was at this time that Tommi learnt about the art of squash, working on the fundamentals that made the Astra Dome sit up and take notice in 2008.
Tommi now lives and plays in England, where he has become a welcome addition to West Totton.

Factfile: Tommi Buckley

D.O.B: 20/05/1983

Strengths: Only man in squash history to carry off the guerilla warfare look

Weaknesses: Lack of 'Big Game Pace' sometimes exposed

Hobbies: Kissing his guns, Rambo movies, Eating small children
