The West Totton Astra Dome:
Venue of Legends



The West Totton Astra Dome is the UK's premier arena for seeing top, top quality players, exhibiting world class squash. Former UK No.1, Peter Nicol is quoted as saying "If I only have one regret in my international career, it's that I never got to play at The West Totton Astra Dome".



Come and see what all the fuss is about. On Thursday evenings there is said to be no hotter venue. Reserve a court on 02380 861712 or come and catch the action at Hazel Farm Road, Totton, Southampton, Hampshire, SO40 8WU.



Since their inception in 1977, Top Trumps have become something of a cult phenomenon, but at no point in their 31 year history have they reached the level of cool, currently associated with them.

Only one set of 'World Class Squash' Top Trumps are still in existence. These are housed in The National Squash Museum and are watched over by 24hr armed guards. squash-news.co.uk now brings you 5 of the best.
